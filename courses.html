Women's Vocational Training Board



    Courses

    Women's Vocational Training Board Impart the following Courses as per the local needs and as and when required to upgrade Vocational Skills among female population.

    We conduct various courses training for three, six and nine months duration under four major categories-
    Sr. Name of the Courses
    1 Information Technology
    2 Home Science
    3 Human Resources Development
    4 Business/Entrepreneurship Development


    Women's Vocational Training Board provide the guidance and training for those interested to run these courses in their area for empowerment of women population. The existing IT institutes/centers or those who are interested to start new center may contact us to become an authorized training partner with the board.
